# Summary Report: "FCAJ Community Day - June 2026"

**FCAJ Community Day - June 2026** was an in-depth workshop for the technology community, focused on updating participants on **AI (Artificial Intelligence)** and **Cloud Computing** trends in real-world environments in Vietnam.

### 1. Event Objectives

The event aimed to create a space for networking and knowledge exchange between technology experts and the AWS user community in Vietnam. It also focused on sharing practical solutions for applying AI and Cloud technologies to business operations.

### 2. Featured Speakers

The event featured several experts from leading technology companies:

- **Steve Trần** from Cloud Thiên Cơ: Shared insights on the mindset for implementing technology solutions.
- **Hiếu Nghị** from Renova Cloud, **Trung** from AWS Study Builder, and **Trung Đ** from R AI: Presented on the topic of Voice AI.
- **Bảo** and **Nguyên** from Cloud Kinetis: Shared the DevOps AI Agent solution.
- **Trường** and **Minh Anh** from Noventis: Presented on AI and human resources.

### 3. Key Highlights

#### Voice AI in enterprise environments

This session discussed Voice AI architecture, how to process Vietnamese voice data, and how to address regional accent challenges in automated customer service systems.

#### DevOps AI Agent

This topic introduced a solution for automating incident handling through AI, helping significantly reduce mean time to resolution (MTTR) for large-scale systems.

#### AI in Human Resource Management

This session introduced Amazon Q as a tool to support HR teams in screening resumes, evaluating candidates, and building automated no-code recruitment workflows.

### 4. Lessons Learned & Work Applications

- **Execution mindset:** I learned about the process from idea and POC to real production deployment, with an emphasis on combining AI tools with specific business problems.
- **Operational optimization:** Engineers can apply DevOps AI Agent to reduce the workload of 24/7 system monitoring and handle incidents more quickly.
- **Department productivity improvement:** Non-technical departments such as HR can use Amazon Q to save time on manual resume screening and reduce subjective errors.

### 5. Event Experience

The event created a highly interactive environment through Q&A sessions between speakers and attendees. Participants did not only listen to theory, but also had the opportunity to experience live demos, hands-on workshops, and special tool trial offers, such as a 2-month trial of Dev AI Agent.

One of the key messages of the event was that **AI does not replace humans**. Instead, it acts as a tool that amplifies human capabilities and helps people focus more on strategic decisions.
